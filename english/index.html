Hume's English contexts — Track 2 comparison materials, kept separate from the La Flèche catalogue.
Claim ceiling. This site presents source texts, computational routing evidence, heatmaps, and review metadata to support scholarly inspection. It does not claim that Hume read, used, borrowed from, or was influenced by any listed text unless a separate human-scholar claim is explicitly stated and documented.

This section covers documented English-language interlocutors and comparators for Hume's work. It offers metadata and comparison tools only: no source texts or passage bodies are published here, and these materials are not La Flèche evidence. Candidate relations are provisional and identify pairings for scholarly inspection — not confirmed contact, reading history, or influence.
