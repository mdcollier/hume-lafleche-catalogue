British Contexts — English comparison and calibration track

British Contexts overviewCatalogueRelation recordsDossiersReview signal mapRetrieval matrix (experimental)

British Contexts is being developed in collaboration with Amyas Merivale, whose English Philosophical Texts Online files provide structured text and paragraph-level metadata for citation, alignment, and review. The full source texts are not republished here.

These are review and routing surfaces for scholarly inspection. They do not establish that Hume read, used, borrowed from, contacted, depended on, or was influenced by any comparison text.

The British comparison catalogue is the main way into this track: browse the comparison texts, their candidate relations, and the passage-bearing dossier previews built from them. British Texts are a comparison and calibration context — not La Flèche holdings, not a Hume reading list, and not evidence of source use or influence.

British Texts catalogue

The British Texts catalogue organizes the English comparison texts used in this track. Text folders gather public text metadata, candidate relations, dossier links, and source/citation readiness where available. British Texts are a comparison and calibration context — not La Flèche holdings, not a Hume reading list, and not a complete British library.
