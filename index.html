Experimental Humanities Project

Computational instruments for studying historical libraries, philosophical comparison, and structured evidence

This site collects scholar-facing materials from an experimental humanities project: source previews, working project texts, catalogue-facing materials, and comparison resources organized for scholarly navigation and review. No claim is made of Humean influence, contact, source-dependence, discovery, validation, recall, or coverage.

Scope & method: this site presents source previews, working project texts, catalogue-facing materials, and scholar-facing navigation. Source texts shown here are OCR-corrected / machine-assisted working project texts — not diplomatic transcriptions and not critical editions; residual OCR or normalization errors may remain. The materials support source readability and scholarly inspection; they do not establish Humean influence, contact, source-dependence, discovery, validation, recall, or coverage.

Earlier experimental interfaces are retained under legacy / experimental materials.
