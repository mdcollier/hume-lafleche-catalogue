Historical Libraries Project

Digital tools for studying early modern intellectual history.
Main current focus
La Flèche / Riboulet Catalogue
Catalogue orientation, approved source previews, method records, and review tools for the Collège de La Flèche / Riboulet library catalogue, associated with Hume's residence at La Flèche in the 1730s.
Open La Flèche project →
Comparison track
British Contexts
A comparison track developed in collaboration with Amyas Merivale, drawing on his English Philosophical Texts Online files, and using the project's architecture to surface candidate comparison relations among Hume, Berkeley, and other early modern British philosophers for scholarly review.
Open British Contexts →
Planned track
American Founders
A planned track using the project's architecture to study how Hume's political and economic essays, and Scottish Enlightenment thought more broadly, may have shaped the writings of the American founders. Not yet operational.
