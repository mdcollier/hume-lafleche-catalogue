The source side of the planned pairing: Scottish Enlightenment authors with openly available scholarly digital editions. The roster is availability-driven. Entries record principal works, digital sources, and preparation status. Listing an author records availability only; it does not assert any relation to any founder.

Availability status: clean_digital = verified open scholarly edition; verify_at_assembly = listed but edition/coverage unconfirmed.

  • Essays, Moral, Political, and Literary (collected 1758; Liberty Fund edition 1985)
