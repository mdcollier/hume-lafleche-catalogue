Experimental humanities tools

An analytical dashboard for the project's experimental-infrastructure layer: reports, methods, review workflows, and catalogue analytics that support scholarly inspection.
Claim ceiling. This site presents source texts, computational routing evidence, heatmaps, and review metadata to support scholarly inspection. It does not claim that Hume read, used, borrowed from, or was influenced by any listed text unless a separate human-scholar claim is explicitly stated and documented.

This is a dashboard over the project's experimental analytical surfaces. Each card carries a state badge. The catalogue is reachable here as one orientation card — it is no longer the destination of this section.

Everything here is metadata-only: it organizes material for scholarly review and does not display source texts or establish Humean influence, contact, source-dependence, reading history, discovery, validation, recall, or coverage. Cards marked in development or blocked pending review are not operational: they describe intended future public-safe surfaces and expose no findings, results, scores, model output, or source passages.
