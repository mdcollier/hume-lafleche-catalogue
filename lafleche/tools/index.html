La Flèche experimental tools

Older La Flèche metadata and comparison tools, kept for scholarly inspection.
These are experimental, metadata-only orientation tools. They do not display source texts and are not part of the current source-preview release. They do not establish Humean influence, contact, source-dependence, discovery, validation, recall, or coverage.
Catalogue coverage map
A metadata-only orientation map showing which Riboulet catalogue sections are represented in current project records and which remain unprocessed. Not an influence map and not a full-catalogue coverage claim.
Review zones
An experimental metadata-only comparison-zone interface for scholarly inspection. It does not display source texts and does not establish influence, contact, source-dependence, discovery, validation, recall, or coverage.
