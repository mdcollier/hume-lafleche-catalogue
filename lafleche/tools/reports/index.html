Scholarly reports and method records

Public-safe summaries of the project's records, grouped by kind.
Claim ceiling. This site presents approved source previews, catalogue materials, computational routing metadata, and review records to support scholarly inspection. It does not claim that Hume read, used, borrowed from, contacted, depended on, or was influenced by any listed text.

Most entries below are method, governance, retrieval, and catalogue-status records — not interpretive scholarly reports. They are grouped by kind so the distinction is clear. The Scholarly reports section lists interpretive summaries as they are cleared for public view; the remaining sections are supporting method and governance records.

Each entry shows a short summary, a public-safe takeaway, and a disposition badge: public-safe summary (a summary of a public-safe record) or summary only (the full working record stays internal). No full report file is linked here: public-safe records are summarized on this page, and the underlying working records — method and governance records, and internal review material — may contain passages, scores, candidate material, or internal review details and are not published here. Nothing on this page states or implies Humean influence, contact, source-dependence, reading history, discovery, validation, recall, or coverage.
