It does not claim that Hume read, used, borrowed from, or was influenced by any listed text unless a separate human-scholar claim is explicitly stated and documented.

This report index lists public-safe summaries of the project's internal method and governance records. Each entry gives a short neutral summary and a public-safe takeaway. The underlying working records may contain passages, scores, candidate material, or internal review details and are not published here; those entries are marked summary only.
