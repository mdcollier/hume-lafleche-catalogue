4 of 2,493 recorded catalogue entries currently have public source-preview pages. This is a source-preview availability measure, not a measure of Humean influence, reading, or historical coverage. "Not yet searched / processed" means an area has not yet been surveyed — it is not a negative finding, and this is not a claim of full-catalogue coverage.

The Riboulet catalogue is organized into named sections (catalogue folders). Only a minority of sections currently carry project records; most of the collection has not yet been surveyed. Use the section cards below to jump to a section's details, then move on to the source previews or the comparison tools.

Where the four source previews sit

All four current previews are project records grouped under the Philosophia review area of the catalogue. Each title links to its source page:

  • Malebranche — Entretiens sur la métaphysique et sur la religion (CL084-17) · surveyed / broad resonance
  • Malebranche — Traité de l'amour de Dieu (CL084-19) · surveyed / broad resonance
  • Burlamaqui — Principes du droit naturel (CL084-07) · surveyed / broad resonance · adjacent / control — not a literal Philosophia-folder entry
  • Babin — Journal (CL080-07) · OCR-needed record

Not every project text is literally in the Philosophia folder: adjacent or control material (such as Burlamaqui) is grouped with the philosophy review area but labeled as adjacent/control.
