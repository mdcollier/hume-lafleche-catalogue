The current public source previews are a small source-readable layer sitting inside one part of this much larger catalogue.

Where the four source previews sit

All four current previews are project records grouped under the Philosophia review area of the catalogue. Their records differ in state:

  • Malebranche — Entretiens (CL084-17) · surveyed / broad resonance
  • Malebranche — Traité de l'amour de Dieu (CL084-19) · surveyed / broad resonance
  • Burlamaqui — Principes du droit naturel (CL084-07) · surveyed / broad resonance · adjacent / control — not a literal Philosophia-folder entry
  • Babin — Journal (CL080-07) · OCR-needed record

Not every project text is literally in the Philosophia folder: adjacent or control material (such as Burlamaqui) is grouped with the philosophy review area but labeled as adjacent/control. Section labels follow the catalogue's own spellings, including variant forms.
