About this project

This project reconstructs the Collège de La Flèche / Riboulet library catalogue as a scholarly working environment for studying the intellectual setting associated with Hume's residence at La Flèche in the 1730s. It combines catalogue orientation, approved source previews, method records, and review-workflow materials.

The public materials are designed for orientation and scholarly inspection; they do not establish what Hume read, used, or drew upon.

This project documents the recorded historical holdings of the Collège de La Flèche / Riboulet library. It is not a record of what Hume read or used, and not the British Contexts comparison track. Record states are project routing/review metadata, not claims of Humean influence, contact, source-dependence, reading, or coverage.
