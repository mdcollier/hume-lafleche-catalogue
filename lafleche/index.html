La Flèche / Riboulet Catalogue

Working source previews and catalogue materials for the library environment at La Flèche.

This section gathers working source previews and catalogue-facing materials related to the Riboulet catalogue of the Collège de La Flèche, where David Hume studied in the 1730s. The aim is to make selected texts easier to inspect, cite, and compare as part of a broader reconstruction of the library environment.

These are working project texts, not critical editions. They may be OCR-corrected, machine-assisted, or normalized for readability. Residual OCR or normalization errors may remain. The site makes no claim of Humean influence, contact, source-dependence, discovery, validation, recall, or coverage.
