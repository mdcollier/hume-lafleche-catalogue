About this project

The Riboulet catalogue is the manuscript library catalogue of the Collège de La Flèche, where David Hume worked in the 1730s. This section reconstructs it as a scholarly working environment. The four current source previews are one small readable layer inside a much larger catalogue — 22 sections and roughly 2,493 entries, most of it not yet surveyed.

Source previews are working project texts — OCR-corrected or normalized for readability, not critical editions; residual errors may remain. The experimental tools are metadata-only. The site makes no claim of Humean influence, contact, source-dependence, discovery, validation, recall, or coverage. Incomplete interfaces and remediation previews are kept in the archive.
