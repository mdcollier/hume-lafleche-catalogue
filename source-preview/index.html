La Flèche catalogue source previews

This index lists the currently available working source previews for the La Flèche / Riboulet catalogue project.
These four source previews are the first public-readable texts in a larger catalogue project — four of roughly 2,493 entries across 22 sections of the Riboulet catalogue. They do not represent the whole catalogue, nor do they imply coverage of Hume's reading or influence. See the catalogue sections & coverage for where they sit. Working project texts, not critical editions; OCR-corrected / machine-assisted and may contain residual errors.
